🎓 CGPA Formula

Grade Point Mapping

O (91-100) → 10 | A+ (81-90) → 9 | A (71-80) → 8 | B+ (61-70) → 7 | B (51-60) → 6 | C+ (41-50) → 5 | C (31-40) → 4 | F (≤30) → 0

Weighted Average CGPA = ROUND(SUM(GradePoint × Credits) / 20, 2)
